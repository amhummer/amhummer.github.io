About

I am a Schmidt Science Fellow in the labs of Profs Emma Lundberg and Wah Chiu at Stanford University. In my research, I am integrating cellular imaging techniques across resolutions to study and model dynamic cellular processes. Prior to my postdoc, I completed my PhD at the University of Oxford, where I developed machine learning models for therapeutic antibody optimization and design. The ultimate aim of my research is to design better therapeutics at reduced timescales and costs.
